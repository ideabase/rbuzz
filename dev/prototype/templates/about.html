About Reading Buzz

Learning is easy with Reading Buzz’s digital content library of educational videos and blog posts. Our content is delivered to schools weekly by MLIS Children’s Librarian.

Facing budget cuts, decreased staff and an evolving educational landscape, school libraries across the country need a solution that is accessible and affordable to supplement their existing services. Reading Buzz addresses this need with a comprehensive educational service offering that includes: weekly lesson plans for school librarians and students, notification of new, trending and must-read books, story time and read-along sessions.

Meeting Lindsey Buzzelli

Plan 2

Lindsey is an accredited Children’s and Young Adult Librarian/School Librarian via Kent State University. Her passion for working with students of all ages and her love of reading inspired her to become a Children’s/Young Adult Librarian. In her library classes, students are encouraged to be hands on: sing, dance, have fun and become inspired by reading and Information Literacy to grow into lifelong learners.
